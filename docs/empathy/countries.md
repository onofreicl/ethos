## Countries that did not sign:

Total: 9

1. Holy See (Vatican City)
  - Status: Permanent UN observer
  - Reason: Chooses not to become a full UN member
  - Internationally recognized sovereign entity
1. State of Palestine
  - Status: Permanent UN observer
  - Recognized by many UN members but not admitted as a full member
  - Therefore not a UN Charter signatory
1. Taiwan (Republic of China)
  - Lost UN membership in 1971
  - Not recognized as a sovereign state by the UN
1. Kosovo
  - Recognized by many countries, but not a UN member
1. Western Sahara
  - Listed by the UN as a non-self-governing territory
1. Northern Cyprus
  - Recognized only by Turkey
1. Abkhazia
  - Limited international recognition
1. South Ossetia
  - Limited international recognition
1. Transnistria
  - Limited international recognition
