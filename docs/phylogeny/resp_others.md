## Declarations

### Ethos is Responsible for Others

- short: `responsible_others`
- long: `I am responsible for the collective actions of others who adopted this ethos. I will make reparations in the proportion that is just.`


### Example

Mahatma Gandhi was fasting many times because of the violence of his fellow Indians during their [Quit India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quit_India_Movement) protests.
